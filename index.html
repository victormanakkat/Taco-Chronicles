About

The Taco Chronicles is a single player action adventure game. You are Dr. Taco, a scientist obsessed with tacos. The goal of the game is to get to Taco Bell safely, without getting shot by crazy monsters that somehow inhabit the game. To earn points, you eliminate monsters, eat tacos, and collect supplies. Tacos give you 7 points, monsters give you 100 points, and supplies give you 25 points. As your total score increases, you unlock various weapons from a 9mm pistol to a flamethrower. Currently there is only one level, but more will be developed soon. Read on to install and play your own copy of the Taco Chronicles.

Install

To download the Taco Chronicles, simply click the download button above and run the installer. Finally, run the exe as and play the game. (You may need to run the exe with admin privileges. To do this, just right click on the file, and click "Run as Administrator" )
